Prompt

Listen carefully to the provided short dialogue and produce a complete temporal audio caption.

Return only the following format and end immediately after </answer>:
<answer>
[start-end] Speaker k: exact spoken content || A concise natural-language description of the audible voice, delivery, emotion, interaction, and relevant background sound.
</answer>

Rules:
- Use one chronological line per speaker turn. Times are seconds from the start of the clip with two decimal places.
- Keep the same anonymous Speaker number for the same voice throughout the clip.
- Transcribe what is actually spoken; do not summarize, paraphrase, invent words, or omit short turns.
- After " || ", describe only audible evidence. Keep it concise but include salient vocal and paralinguistic detail such as tone, pace, hesitation, emphasis, emotion, voice quality, interaction, and background sound when present.
- Do not force attributes that cannot be heard and do not guess identity, age, gender, accent, or setting.
- A clearly audible non-speech event that matters may use Speaker label "Sound Event".
- Every line must contain the timestamp, speaker label, spoken content, and description separated by " || ".

Perfect format example:
<answer>
[00.35-02.10] Speaker 1: Good morning, how can I help you? || A clear, calm voice opens politely at a moderate pace; the line is otherwise quiet.
[02.42-04.85] Speaker 2: Hi, I'd like to check my account balance. || A second voice responds evenly, with a brief hesitation after the greeting and faint call-line noise underneath.
[05.18-06.70] Speaker 1: Of course. || The first speaker answers promptly in a reassuring, professional tone.
</answer>
