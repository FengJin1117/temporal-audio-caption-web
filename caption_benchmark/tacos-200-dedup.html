Metric Notes

Event-F1: one-to-one semantic matching, then true positives require onset error within ±1.0s.

Segment-F1: frame-level active/inactive scoring at 100ms resolution.

Hall%: default approximation where semantically unmatched predictions are counted as hallucinations.
